The Almaty Mountains

Nestled in southeastern Kazakhstan, the Almaty mountains form part of the stunning Trans-Ili Alatau range. Known for their snow-covered peaks, alpine meadows, and crystal-clear rivers, these mountains offer breathtaking scenery year-round.

Visitors can enjoy hiking trails, skiing in winter resorts like Shymbulak, and exploring the famous Big Almaty Lake. The mountains are not just a natural wonder but also a cultural treasure, deeply rooted in the heritage of the Kazakh people.
